    Subcommands¶

    Subcommands are a more advanced form of creating bots, and it's recommended that you read the discord documentation before coming here. Subcommands follow a similar structure as standard commands.

    Info

    Notice the structure, it's more "nested". The Discord documentation has a diagram explaining the correct structure.
